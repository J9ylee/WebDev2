Sexism in Gaming

I have always loved playing video games since I was a little girl. From my pink Nintendo to my pink PC, I learned that the gaming community isn’t welcoming to girls. I was not welcome to jump in on a conversation about a video game because I was either lying or bad at the game. I felt like I was always put in a position to prove myself, but gaming shouldn’t be like that. Why can’t I freely play games as a form of entertainment without people judging me?

The toxic gaming culture leaves women vulnerable, not being able to freely play games with no worries...

Many women seek video games as a form of entertainment or to relax, and it's being taken away from them...
