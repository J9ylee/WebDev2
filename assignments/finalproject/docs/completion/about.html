About Us

User Research

Key Findings

  • Frequency of Use: 60%+ use skincare daily; a smaller group uses it rarely.
  • Product Selection Methods:
    • Online reviews & word-of-mouth
    • Ingredient research
    • Dermatologist recommendations
  • Ingredient Awareness:
    • Some always check labels; many only occasionally.
    • A subset unsure about specific ingredient risks.

Skin Concerns & Challenges

  • Common issues: acne, hyperpigmentation, sensitivity.
  • Over 50% experienced redness, irritation, or breakouts.
  • Fears: harmful products, high prices, understanding ingredient effects.
  • Seasonal skin changes add complexity.

Preferred Solutions

  • Tools to analyze ingredient safety.
  • Personalized recommendations & dermatologist guidance.
  • Interest in sustainability and ethical sourcing.

Research Articles

Dermatologists stress the importance of identifying allergens and understanding ingredient interactions. Ryczaj et al. (2023) found eczema treatments often contain food allergens, raising sensitization risks on inflamed skin. Similarly, Hiranput et al. (2023) showed “hypoallergenic” labels can be misleading, so an educational component is critical.

Goh et al. (2024) recommend a structured routine—cleansing, treatment, moisturization, sun protection—for acne, rosacea, atopic dermatitis, and sensitive skin. Machine learning approaches (Nithyadevi, 2024; Lin et al., 2022) can further personalize suggestions based on skin characteristics. Ethical sourcing (Ajayi et al., 2024) not only meets consumer values but promotes environmental responsibility.

Competitive Analysis & Ideation

Competitors

  • SkinSAFE: large ingredient database, personalized filters, barcode scanning. Strengths: Comprehensive data. Weaknesses: Sparse educational content.
  • Think Dirty: toxicity scores, community ratings. Strengths: Clear “clean” focus. Weaknesses: Low personalization; controversial metric.
  • INCIDecoder: ingredient breakdown with research-backed ratings. Strengths: Deep education. Weaknesses: Limited routine recommendations.

Market Gaps

  • Deeper personalization by skin condition, goals, lifestyle.
  • More holistic routine recommendations (not just ratings).
  • Rich educational modules on ingredient interactions.
  • Community features (diaries, testimonials, peer routines).

Ideation

  • AI-Driven Personalization Unique daily routines based on user profile + product analysis.
  • Ingredient Education Interactive modules explaining sourcing, efficacy, interactions.
  • Clean, Intuitive UI Minimal design with “skin diary” tracking.
  • Community Hub Share routines, progress, tips—and learn from peers.
